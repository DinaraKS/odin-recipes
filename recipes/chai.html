What is Lemon Tea?

It is a simple beverage that will surely refresh you as soon as you take a sip. To make this easy and quick tea, green tea is boiled in the water for a few minutes and then strained in a cup. Add lemon juice, honey, and give it a mix. Your tea is ready! You can even add in some mint leaves while boiling the tea, it adds a great flavor and makes your tea even more refreshing. You can even make Lemon Iced Tea, just let the strained water come to room temperature, add in some ice cubes and then add in some lemon juice and honey.

  • Water
  • Lemon Juice
  • Honey

    Step By Step Recipe

    1. Heat water in the pan. Once the water comes to a boil, simmer the heat. Add tea leaves and let them steep for a minute.
    2. Strain the tea in serving cups.
    3. Add lemon juice and honey and stir well. Serve immediately
